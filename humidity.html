City Lat vs Humidity (%)

This scatter plot shows a weak negative non-linear correlation between City Lat. and Humidity.
In the Plot, it is difficult establish a relationship as the plot doesn't have a distinct shape.
But I can observe that cities closer to the equator tend to generally have higher humidity. There looks to be a skew to the right, as cities that are closer to the equartor on the negative side(-20-0), are more concentrated around the 100% humidity level. Cities with a lower positive Lat tend to be more spread about(0-20).
I will note that there seems to be some outliers in the +60 to 80 degree range that rest at 0% humidity.
